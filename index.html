Guiding Principles of NUA

Association Foundation

NUA is formed by members with the shared commitment to foster a transparent, fair, and harmonious framework for community welfare and governance.

Core Pillars

  • Accountability in decision-making and financial management
  • Inclusiveness in representation and participation
  • Transparency in operations, communications, records and reports
  • Harmony through proactive conflict prevention and members consensus

Modernization Goals

  • Clear, agreed and enforceable rules and regulations
  • Digital systems for administration, payments, disclosures, and voting
  • Participatory processes through open communication channels and discussion forums

Our Vision

A vision that prioritizes enforceable governance, member education, and dispute-free administration through transparent and well-defined processes and procedures.
