Welcome, MC Member!

This is your centralized dashboard for managing NUA operations securely and efficiently.

Available Modules

👤 Me Details Module

Quickly access your personal profile, download your membership documents, and review all invoices assigned to your plot.

👥 Member Module

Access the comprehensive member directory, view detailed profiles, property information, and efficiently onboard new association members.

📁 Document Module

Securely browse, view, and upload important association documents, including NUA Bye-laws and restricted financial records.

🏛️ Communication & Meetings

Log and review meeting minutes, track sent letters, and perform bulk mail merges for mass communication to members.

💰 Invoice Module

Track the welfare collection status, raise new invoices, and send receipts to members for association dues.

📊 MC Action Items

Prioritize, assign, and track the completion status of essential tasks and operational objectives for the Managing Committee.

📝 Vendor Payment Module

Securely submit new payment vouchers and review the detailed list of historical vendor payments for full financial transparency.

🛒 PO Module

Manage Purchase Orders effectively by submitting new POs and tracking approved expenditures from start to finish.

📈 Reporting Module

Generate analytical summaries and comprehensive reports to assess the operational health of the association. (Coming soon)

💡 Pro Navigation Tips

Need more space? Use the "⛶ Expand View" button at the top left of the sidebar to hide the website header and switch to fullscreen mode. This is highly recommended when viewing large spreadsheets or long documents!

Reading on a smaller screen? Use the "☰ Toggle Menu" button to collapse the sidebar entirely, giving your current tool maximum width.

Select a module from the left menu to get started.
