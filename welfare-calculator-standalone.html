The NUA Mathematical Model

Unlike the old system which charged purely on Plot Size (Sq.ft. × 12), NUA enforces an equitable mathematical model prioritizing operational realistic costs and fairness between plot owners and residents.

The Formula

Base Contribution: ₹1,250 / Month per owner
Shared Maintenance Cost: Additional Expenditure ÷ ( (3 × Residences) + Plots )
  • Plot Charge Per Year: (Base Contribution × 12) + Shared Cost
  • Residence Charge Per Year: (Base Contribution × 12) + (3 × Shared Cost)

Why is the Residence Weight 3x?

Residences utilize significantly more resources like STP, security coverage, regular waste disposal, and community water compared to empty vacant plots. The 1:3 ratio is considered an industry best practice among leading communities to ensure fairness.

Key Benefits & Improvements

  • Eliminates hidden costs and unexplained overheads.
  • Provides a completely transparent breakdown of charges.
  • Cost Optimization: The per-member cost structurally decreases as more members construct homes and move in!
